Teaching

Jun 13, 2026
1 minute read

I train students in molecular labs, transcriptomics and phylogenomic data analysis, support them with their research projects, and now work as a teaching assistant guiding practical classes and helping them build confidence in the lab.

My Teaching Responsibilities:

• Demonstrating laboratory techniques (biochemical tests, PCR setup, biosafety)

• Guiding students through experimental workflows

• Preparing teaching materials, flowcharts, and troubleshooting

• Supervising small groups during practical sessions

• Supporting students with data interpretation and report writing
